Subnetting is Fun!

Here is the new, all-Javascript version of my original web-based quiz to practice your subnetting skills. Given an IP and a netmask, you derive the network and broadcast addresses. I wrote it to prep for the CCNA exam and to sharpen my on-the-job subnetting skills. I recommend reading Chris Bloomfield's Subnetting Made Easy that gives an excellent and mercifully short explanation of how to subnet.

If you're interested, the source code is available on Github, and you can read my original post about it. This new version (10 years later!) is all Javascript, Angular.js, and Bootstrap--no more backend PHP/Apache server! I motivated to rewrite it so I could move my blog to Amazon's static website hosting through S3. If you find any bugs or have feedback, submit an issue or drop me an email. Good luck and have fun!
